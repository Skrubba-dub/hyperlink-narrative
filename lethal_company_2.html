During mission 6 you need to go to the bathroom, so you stay on the ship while your teammates find more items. When you come back, you notice that nobody has returned yet. You check the ship's radar cameras but nobody is moving. Usually this means you should lauch the ship, but this is the final day to reach quota and your crew does not have enough money to win. You decide be brave and enter the warehouse hoping to find your crewmates and some loot.
